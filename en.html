Whats gonna happen?

In the English section, students present projects on grammar, storytelling, vocabulary, and famous authors. Creative writing pieces and reading activities highlight how English helps us communicate globally. This section encourages confidence in speaking, reading, and expressing ideas.

Venue:- ,5thTheta 6thE

Building Blocks of Language : Parts of Speech

Description:-This interactive model showcases the nine parts of speech in English grammar, helping visitors understand their roles and functions in sentence construction.

The Article Adventure

Description:- An interactive model showcasing the usage of articles and helping people understand the use in different contexts.

Verb Vault

Description:- A model showcasing various verbs through miniature models

Preposition Playground

Description:- A working model portraying different prepositions through a playground

Tense Timeline

Description:- This title suggests a fun and interactive way to explore verb tenses, with the idea of traveling through time to learn about different tenses.

Story Builder

Description:- An indulging game where players arrange story cards in chronological order to form a coherent narrative, promoting reading comprehension and storytelling skills.

Word Match - Antonyms/ synonyms

Description:-A vocabulary game where players match words with their antonyms and synonyms, enhancing their understanding of word meanings and relationships.

Diamante Delight

Description:- A creative activity where participants build diamante poems, exploring word choice and poetic structure to create visually appealing and meaningful poems.

Pronounce it Right

Description:- Test your pronunciation skills with commonly mispronounced words like "receipt," "data," and "pronounce." Can you get them right?

Book Nook/Café

Description:- A cozy book corner featuring a curated collection of popular and classic books, inviting visitors to explore, read, and discover new literary worlds.
