About Kalpotsav – Ekayanaa School’s Annual Exhibition

Welcome to Kalpotsav, the signature annual exhibition of Ekayanaa School, where learning meets creativity and knowledge comes to life beyond the classroom.

Kalpotsav is a multidisciplinary celebration that showcases the academic achievements, artistic talents, and innovative spirit of our students. Rooted in the belief that education should be both experiential and expressive, this exhibition brings together students from all grades to present projects and performances across various subjects including Science, Mathematics, Social Studies, Languages, Information Technology, and Visual & Performing Arts.

What Makes Kalpotsav Unique?

  • Student-Led Exploration: From designing models to live experiments, students lead the charge with creativity and confidence.
  • Interdisciplinary Learning: Projects connect academic subjects with real-world themes like sustainability, technology, and culture.
  • Community Involvement: Parents, teachers, and guests are invited to engage, explore, and celebrate student work.
  • A Celebration of Talent: In addition to academic exhibits, Kalputsav features music, dance, storytelling, and art displays.

At Ekayanaa School, we believe that education is a journey of discovery. Kalputsav embodies that philosophy—offering students a chance to think, create, collaborate, and communicate in meaningful ways.

We invite you to be a part of this enriching experience and witness the brilliance and potential of our young minds.
